Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of electronic products do you offer?
We specialize in a wide range of electronics, including consumer gadgets (smartphones, tablets, wearables), home appliances, computer components, audio-visual equipment, and DIY electronic parts (sensors, microcontrollers, etc.). Visit our Product Categories page for a full list.

2. How can I place an order on your website?
Simply browse through our products, add the items you want to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be asked to provide shipping and payment details. Once confirmed, you’ll receive an order confirmation email with a tracking link.

3. Do you offer warranty on your products?
Yes. Most of our products come with a standard 12-month manufacturer’s warranty. Some high-end items may include extended warranty options. Specific warranty information is available on each product page.

4. What is your return and refund policy?
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy on most products. If the item is unused and in its original packaging, you can initiate a return from your account dashboard. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after product inspection.

5. How long will it take to receive my order?
Shipping usually takes 2–5 business days, depending on your location. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout. You can track your order status anytime via the link sent to your email.

6. Is it safe to shop on your website?
Absolutely. Our website uses SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure your data is protected. We never store your payment information.

7. Can I get technical support for a product I purchased?
Yes. Our customer support team is available to assist you with any technical issues. You can contact us through live chat, email, or phone. Some products also include access to detailed manuals and troubleshooting guides.

8. Do you provide bulk orders or B2B pricing?
Yes, we support bulk orders and offer special pricing for businesses, schools, and resellers. Please reach out to our B2B Sales Team at b2b@yourelectro.com or fill out our Bulk Inquiry Form.

9. What if the item I want is out of stock?
If a product is out of stock, you can click "Notify Me" on the product page to receive an alert when it becomes available again. You can also contact us for expected restock dates.
